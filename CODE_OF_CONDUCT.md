# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in Kody a welcoming, safe, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of contribution.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code rather than people.
- Assume good intent while accepting responsibility for impact.
- Give actionable feedback and make space for different perspectives.
- Respect privacy, security embargoes, and project boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized language or imagery, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and publication of private information without permission are not acceptable in any project space.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and made without retaliation.

For a conduct concern, contact the maintainers privately through the repository's [security advisory form](https://github.com/jianliang00/kody/security/advisories/new) and state that the report concerns community conduct rather than a software vulnerability. Reports will be handled with discretion; confidentiality cannot be guaranteed when safety or legal obligations require escalation.

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, review discussions, community spaces, and public representation of the project.
